## 2. Revenue Streams

- **Setup fee**: One-time per site or per tenant (installation, config, training).
- **Annual subscription**: SaaS fee per tier; billed annually (discount) or monthly.
- **Add-on modules**: Extra cameras above tier, SMS credits, extra sites.
- **Hardware margin**: Edge units, relays, sirens sold at margin.
- **SaaS margin**: Recurring revenue after cloud and support cost.

## 4. Unit Economics (Illustrative)

- **Basic**: Hardware margin ~25%; SaaS margin ~75% after cloud cost.
- **Professional**: Higher ARPU; support cost ~10% of revenue.
- **Enterprise**: Custom; target 70%+ gross margin; dedicated support cost in contract.

## 5. License Enforcement (Recap)

- **14-day trial**: Full functionality offline; one per tenant.
- **Activation key**: Converts trial or new tenant to paid; binds devices and max phones.
- **Expiry**: Renewal required; grace and then feature restriction as per [02-license-engine.md](../security/02-license-engine.md).
