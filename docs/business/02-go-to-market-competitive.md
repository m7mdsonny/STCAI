# Go-to-Market & Competitive Strategy

## 1. Target Segments

| Segment | Pain points | Fit |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| **Manufacturing / factories** | Fire risk, theft, compliance, insurance | Core: fire + theft; multi-site for groups |
| **Warehouses & logistics** | Theft, intrusion, fire; existing cameras | Works on existing RTSP cameras |
| **Industrial parks** | Multi-tenant sites; central monitoring | Multi-site + roles |
| **Insurance-linked** | Risk scoring, compliance, premiums | Risk analytics + insurance reports |
| **Retail / retail back-office** | Theft, after-hours intrusion | Theft + schedule |

## 2. Pain Points Addressed

- **“We have cameras but no intelligence”**: Add AI on top of existing RTSP; no rip-and-replace.
- **“We need response in seconds”**: Edge siren <500 ms; no cloud round-trip for alarm.
- **“We have no or bad internet”**: 100% offline edge; 14-day trial without internet.
- **“We need proof for insurance”**: Events, clips, risk score, compliance reports.
- **“We need one app for all sites”**: Multi-site, OTP login, roles, escalation.

## 3. Sales Process

1. **Lead**: Inbound (web, demo request) or outbound (target accounts).
2. **Qualification**: Site count, camera count, fire/theft priority, budget.
3. **14-day live demo**: Deploy edge at site (or pilot site); no payment; full fire + optional theft; show siren, app, risk.
4. **Proposal**: Tier + setup + annual; add-ons (cameras, SMS).
5. **Activation**: Contract; activation key; provisioning; go-live.
6. **Success**: Onboarding; first risk report; renewal reminder.

## 4. 14-Day Live Demo Strategy

- **Goal**: Prove value on their premises with their cameras.
- **Delivery**: Install edge unit; connect 1–5 cameras; enable fire (and theft if relevant); arm siren.
- **No internet required**: Trial works offline; sync when available for app and cloud dashboard.
- **Handover**: Demo app (OTP signup); show event, snapshot, clip, acknowledge.
- **Conversion**: Before day 14, proposal; activation key on signature.

## 5. Insurance Partnerships

- **Value to insurer**: Risk visibility; fewer claims; compliance evidence.
- **Model**: Referral or co-brand; discount for insureds; or white-label risk report.
- **Deliverable**: Standardized risk score and compliance report (export PDF/API).

## 6. Channel Partners

- **Integrators**: Security and industrial automation; they deploy edge and sell subscription.
- **Distributors**: Hardware + license distribution in region.
- **Model**: Partner margin on setup + recurring share or referral fee.

## 7. Case Study Strategy

- **Pilot factories (Q1)**: 3 sites; document before/after (response time, false alarms, insurance interaction).
- **Metrics**: Time to alarm, false positive rate, theft/fire incidents; quote from safety manager.
- **Format**: 1-pager + video; use in Egypt, GCC, Africa campaigns.

## 8. Competitive Positioning

### Vs. Hikvision / Dahua AI

| Dimension | Us | Them |
|-----------|----|------|
| **Existing cameras** | Works on any RTSP | Often lock-in to their cameras |
| **Offline** | Full edge; trial offline | Often cloud-dependent |
| **Automation** | Siren/relay <500 ms; industrial | Varies |
| **SaaS** | Multi-tenant, license, app | Often NVR-centric |
| **Risk scoring** | Core product | Less focus |
| **Industrial logic** | Zones, schedule, escalation | More generic |

### Vs. Traditional Fire Alarm

| Dimension | Us | Them |
|-----------|----|------|
| **Detection** | AI vision (smoke/flame) | Sensors (smoke/heat) |
| **Coverage** | Camera FOV | Point sensors |
| **Theft** | Yes | No |
| **Analytics** | Risk, clips, reports | Limited |
| **Integration** | Relay to panel possible | We can trigger panel |

### Vs. Generic CCTV

| Dimension | Us | Them |
|-----------|----|------|
| **Intelligence** | Fire + theft AI on edge | Recording only or cloud AI |
| **Response** | Local siren/relay | Manual or none |
| **Offline** | Yes | Often no |
| **Risk** | Score + reports | No |

### Differentiation Summary

- **Works on existing cameras** (RTSP).
- **Offline-first edge** with 14-day trial without internet.
- **Smart automation**: local siren in <500 ms; relays; MQTT/Modbus.
- **SaaS analytics**: risk score, dashboards, insurance reports.
- **Industrial-grade**: zones, schedules, escalation, multi-tenant.
- **One platform**: fire + theft + app + cloud in one license.
