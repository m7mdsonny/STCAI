# Brand Positioning

## 1. Positioning Statement

**For** industrial and commercial site operators and safety managers  
**Who** need immediate fire and theft response and proof for insurance,  
**Our platform** is an industrial AI risk intelligence system  
**That** runs fully on-site for sub-second alarm and works offline, while giving cloud analytics and mobile control.  
**Unlike** traditional fire-only systems or cloud-only CCTV AI,  
**We** combine edge AI, local automation, and SaaS in one licensed platform that works with existing cameras.

## 2. Key Messages

- **Sub-second response**: Siren triggers in under 500 ms—no cloud round-trip.
- **Works offline**: 14-day trial and full detection without internet; sync when connected.
- **Use your cameras**: Add AI to existing RTSP cameras; no rip-and-replace.
- **Fire + theft in one**: One platform, one app, one license for safety and security.
- **Risk intelligence**: Daily risk score, insurance-ready reports, and escalation logic.
- **Industrial-grade**: Zones, schedules, relays, MQTT/Modbus, built for factories and warehouses.

## 3. Tone

- Professional, precise, safety-focused.
- Avoid consumer or gaming language.
- Emphasize reliability, compliance, and control.

## 4. Audience

- **Primary**: Plant/site managers, safety officers, security managers.
- **Secondary**: CFO (insurance, cost of incidents); insurers (risk visibility).
- **Channel**: Integrators, distributors, industrial automation partners.

## 5. Naming and Tagline (Options)

- **Product name**: e.g. “RiskIntel Edge” / “Industrial Risk Intelligence Platform”.
- **Tagline**: e.g. “Fire and theft intelligence at the edge.” / “See risk. Act in milliseconds.”
